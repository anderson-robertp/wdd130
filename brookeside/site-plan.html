Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to advertise the final session of a role playing game campaign to be streamed over Twitch and other platforms. The goal is to get as many people watching as possible to share in the experience.

Audience

The audience for this webpage is anyone who enjoys watching live-play roleplaying games. This would typicallay be someone from the age of 12 to 35 age range.

Home page

The Brookside Exploration Society is reaching the finale of their years-long quest to end the threat of Tiamat and the Cult of the Dragon. This a live play ttrpg stream that is sure to entertain. Everyone is welcome: from the noob to the veteran. Come watch as the society tries to thwart Severin and the cult as they attempt to summon Tiamat and begin the Tyranny of Dragons!

The Story so Far

History of the Brookeside Exploration Society The BES initially formed to explore places where its members have never been before. Founded by famed explorer Renault de Wathes as he retired from his storied career to keep the adventure alive, many members are simply looking for a cure for their wanderlust and a safe way to travel. This group was exploring around the town of Greenest when a blue dragon attacked. After fighting their way to safety, the group was asked to help the townsfolk find safety. Having found a common foe in the invaders, the group went on to investigate a local cave system that served as the camp for the Cult of the Dragon. There they found evidence of a plan for some sort of massive undertaking involving the theft of massive amounts of treasure. Each armed with their own reason to investigate, the group traveled up the sword coast
