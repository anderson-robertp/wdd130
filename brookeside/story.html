The Story So Far

History of the Brookeside Exploration Society

The BES initially formed to explore places where its members have never been before. Founded by famed explorer Renault de Wathes as he retired from his storied career to keep the adventure alive, many members are simply looking for a cure for their wanderlust and a safe way to travel.

Greenest

This group was exploring around the town of Greenest when a blue dragon attacked. After fighting their way to safety, the group was asked to help the townsfolk find safety.

The Cult of the Dragon

Having found a common foe in the invaders, the group went on to investigate a local cave system that served as the camp for the Cult of the Dragon. There they found evidence of a plan for some sort of massive undertaking involving the theft of massive amounts of treasure.
